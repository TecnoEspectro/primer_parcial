    Short description


    Devil May Cry 3 is a hack n slash developed and distributed by Capcom.

    The game tells the history of the origins from Dante the demon hunter, who is assaulted by Arkam, a Vergils ally, to make Dante seek for Vergil stop him and figth him.
    The history has impacting moments, is entertaining and enjoyable.

    The game has high playability and is fun, having a score system that appears in the figths and another one in the end mission screen.

    The player has variety of weapons and powers, like swords, guns, devil trigger, movesets and Styles.

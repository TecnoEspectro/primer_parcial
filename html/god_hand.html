    Short description


    Metal Gear Rising Revengueance is a hack n slash developed by PlatinumGames and distributed by Konami.

    The game tells the history of Raiden that works in a private army, his job is mostly to defeat terrorists and armyes, but this time he confronts a cyborg army, the Desperado team.
    The history has cool moments, filosofic themes, good sountrack, memorable characters; most of all the history is entertaining and enjoyable.

    The game has high playability and is fun, having a score system that appears in the end mission screen and zaindatsu mode (katana mode), opcionaly stealth, ripper mode (or Jack mode) and dinamic boss fights.

    The player has variety of weapons, like swords, guns, items, movesets and ripper mode.
